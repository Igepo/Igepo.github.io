Peak

Peak is a personal prototype. It is an FPS game where the environment includes procedural elements.

I wanted to use procedural animations for this project. The goal is to be able to use the knowledge acquired on this prototype to create bosses that would move procedurally.

This allowed me to learn :

- The use of inverse kinematics and how to apply it on models imported from Blender.

- The use of raycast to determine the next step. Here the position of the next step is indicated by the green sphere.

- The use of the "coroutine" functions that were used here to create the leg movement during the whole trip rather than making it "appear" at the next position.
